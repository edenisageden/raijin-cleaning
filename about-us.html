Raijin Cleaning

Welcome to Raijin Cleaning, Queenstown's premier property cleaning service. Born and raised in Queenstown, cleanliness and comfort have always been fundamental to my values. At just eighteen, I embarked on a journey to establish a business dedicated to transforming living and working spaces. What started as a passion project has blossomed into Queenstown's leading property cleaning service, committed to excellence and customer satisfaction.

With a deep-rooted understanding of our community's needs and a relentless dedication to quality, we take pride in delivering exceptional residential and commercial cleaning solutions. Whether it's restoring the brilliance of your windows, maintaining pristine office environments, or ensuring your home is a sanctuary, we are here to exceed your expectations.

Join us in making every space in Queenstown shine bright with Raijin Cleaning.
